Siberian Husky

The Siberian Husky is a family oriented dog that travels in packs.They a super friendly and playful.

  • Eye Shape: Almond
  • Eye Color: Brown or Blue
  • Weight: No more than 30 kg
  • Strongest Trait: They have powerful agile legs

These dogs do not make great watch dogs because they are overlly friendly. Also they are super playful and loves to chase small animals so be sure to have them in a safely contained area so they do not run off. They will make the best family pet.
